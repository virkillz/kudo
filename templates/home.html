Myanmar experimental crypto token

We have created 1 billion units of a digital token we call “Kudos” (plural of Kudo) to distribute for fun, learning and organizational expediency at the UniHack Challenge 3, which his focused this year on blockchain technology. Our aim is to familiarize people with crypto-economics in as simple way as possible with an interactive ‘network’ experience. Build to be a catalyst for Myanmar blockchain developer

There are many approaches to introduce blockchain and a digital token ecosystem in business. We have created Kudo on a permissioned blockchain (one that requires permission to join the node) to simplify the security. It will also create a controlled environment where people can start to collaborate and see what they can do in the ecosystem.

Faster confirmation

Unlike 10 minutes in Bitcoin, Kudo confirmation time is 40 times fasters.

Free transaction fee

Since this is built on the permissioned blockchain, we can set zero transaction fee and able to handle spam at the same time.

Bitcoin compatibility

We have backward compatibility with bitcoin, which means you can use existing libraries to mess around with.

How it works?

1.
Create wallet

Generate a paper wallet from here. Later on you can use different type of wallet like mobile wallet but paper wallet is the safest one as long as you keep the private key secret.

2.
Get Kudo

Now get some Kudo! There are several ways to get Kudo. The easiest way is from Faucet or join our hackathon. Later on you can run a node server and get daily Kudo, doing several reward program, or simply ask from others.

3.
Send to others

There is a reason we name it Kudo. So you can use it as a token of support or appreciation to other people in Myanmar Blockchain community. This is our way to encourage community while learning.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

  • What can I use with my Kudo?

    You can store it on your wallet, send it to people. That's it. Treat it as a 'like' in facebook. But you have limited 'like' and there are limited amount of 'like' in existence.

    But who know someday someone are willing to pay to get your Kudo or you can exchange to many cool stuff with your Kudo. We are preparing some stuff too.

  • Does it have any value? Should I pay for it?

    No. Currently we are creating several program as means to give away Kudo for Free. However this is limited up to 1 billion Kudo to be distributed. So eventhough it is free, Kudo is limited asset.

    Its not impossible when someday people offer something in exchange of X amount of Kudo, then it is start getting its natural value based on people's demand.

  • What makes Kudo special?

    Kudo is a cryptoasset reside on blockchain, which means it cannot be duplicated. Cannot be transferred to other address without posession of private keys, unlike other digital things who can be easily duplicated or modifed by database administrator or anyone who have access to database.

    What makes Kudo special is its free transaction cost. To be sustainable, almost all of cryptocurrency require transaction fee, while Kudo transaction fee by nature is zero.

  • Can I get Kudo by mining? Just like bitcoin or ethereum?

    Kudo is 100% premined. It is common in certain cryptocoin such as NANO (previously XRB). Mining Kudo is restricted to trusted node due to its permissioned nature (big cryptocurrency such as XRP also use this method) only as means to seal the transaction block and no Kudo reward is acquired.

    You can get certain amount of Kudo (by this time is 10.000/day) simply by running a node.

  • What happened if I lost my private key? How to secure my Kudo?

    If you lose your private key, your Kudo is gone. That's it. Due to the cryptographic nature, there's no way you can get your private key if you forget or lose it. Which means you cannot send your Kudo to anyone. We don't store your private key in our database. This is to ensure the security. In pricipal, private key is THE proof of ownership of a Kudo in a particular address.

    To save a Kudo. You can split into several address. Ensure that you not store it digitally in computer without encryption and never share it with anyone. If you need to share, create a wallet and transfer to that amount. Create new wallet is very easy and fast.
